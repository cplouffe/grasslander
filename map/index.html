How to setup your farm:

Step 1: Use the search bar find your farm using an address, or approximate location and then pan the map to your area.

Step 2: Identify your property parcel and click it. If you have multiple parcels you can click the "Add More" button at the end.

Step 3: Fill in the form with the information about your farm and submit.

Step 4: If you are finished with attributing the land parcels that belong to your farmstead you can click "Proceed".

Please use the tool in the top left corner to draw each of your fields

Draw a polygon around each of your fields individually. Fill out the form and submit. If you have multiple fields please click the "Add More" button at the end.

Please select what kind of activity you would like to add.

Bird Observations: Please drop a point using the tool in the top left corner of where you were when you observed to bird. Fill out the form and submit.

To Add Field Activities: Please drop a point using the tool in the top left corner in the field you would like to add an activity to. Fill out the form and submit.
